## 🎨 COPYWRITING STYLE REFERENCE

### DO: ✅
- Short sentences
- Power words: "Selesai", "Lancar", "Expert", "Garansi", "Sekarang"
- Direct questions ("Ini masalah kamu, kan?")
- Conversational tone (kamu, kami, mereka)
- Urgency triggers (deadline, terbatas, sekarang)
- Social proof numbers (150+, 20+, 100%)
- Micro-moments (< 5 menit, 48 jam)

### DON'T: ❌
- Corporate jargon
- Generic benefits
- Passive voice
- Long paragraphs
- Unsupported claims
- Basa-basi intro
