## 💡 PRO TIPS

**TIP 1: Rotate Creative Weekly**
Ad fatigue happens after 7-14 days. Refresh images/videos weekly.

**TIP 2: Use Video for Higher CTR**
Video ads typically get 20-30% higher CTR than images.

**TIP 3: Test Urgency Component**
Slots limited, deadline approaching → drives conversions.

**TIP 4: Keep Messaging Consistent**
Ad headline → Landing page headline match = higher conversion.

**TIP 5: WhatsApp Response Speed Matters**
Respond < 5 minutes = 70% lead conversion rate.
Wait > 1 hour = 20% lead conversion rate.

**TIP 6: Segment Your Audience**
Students vs Corporate = different messaging.
Age 20-25 vs 25-30 = different pain points.

**TIP 7: Use Testimonials Liberally**
People trust other people more than brand promises.
Include as many testimonials as possible.
