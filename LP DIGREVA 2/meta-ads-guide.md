## 🚨 TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### ISSUE: High CTR but Low Conversion
**Causes:**
- Landing page not mobile-friendly
- Unclear messaging
- WhatsApp link broken
- Wrong audience

**Solution:**
- Audit landing page mobile experience
- Simplify above-the-fold copy
- Test WhatsApp link directly
- Refine audience targeting

### ISSUE: Low CTR (< 4%)
**Causes:**
- Ad copy not compelling
- Image/video not eye-catching
- Wrong audience
- Ad fatigue

**Solution:**
- Test new headlines
- Create fresh creative
- Expand audience targeting
- Rotate ad creative weekly

### ISSUE: High Cost Per Lead (> $5)
**Causes:**
- Low conversion rate on landing page
- High bounce rate
- Wrong audience
- Poor ad/landing page match

**Solution:**
- Improve landing page conversion rate
- A/B test landing page elements
- Refine audience
- Check message consistency (ad → landing)

## 💡 BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ✅ Test one variable at a time
- ✅ Let campaigns run minimum 3-7 days before judging
- ✅ Use video for higher engagement
- ✅ Rotate creative every 7-14 days (prevent fatigue)
- ✅ Monitor WhatsApp chat quality
- ✅ Follow up on WhatsApp leads within 5 minutes
- ✅ Segment retargeting audience

### DON'T:
- ❌ Change bidding strategy mid-campaign
- ❌ Pause ads based on 1-2 days data
- ❌ Use generic stock photos
- ❌ Make vague CTAs ("Learn More")
- ❌ Use too many CTA buttons
- ❌ Ignore negative feedback in comments
- ❌ Copy competitor ads directly
